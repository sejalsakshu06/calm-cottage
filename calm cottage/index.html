About Us

Welcome to calm cottage

Calm Cottage in Barog, Himachal Pradesh, is a charming retreat nestled amidst the serene beauty of the Himalayas.allowing visitors to unwind and reconnect with nature.Surrounded by lush green forests and stunning mountain vistas. Its cozy and well-appointed rooms, adorned with local decor and modern amenities, ensure a comfortable stay for guests. Visitors can indulge in leisurely walks along scenic trails, breathing in the crisp mountain air and enjoying the breathtaking views. The nearby attractions like Barog Railway Station and Choor Chandni Peak add to the allure of the location. In the evenings, guests can gather around a bonfire and relish the warmth while sharing stories and experiences. The cottage's friendly staff, known for their hospitality, create a homely atmosphere that makes the stay even more memorable. Whether you seek a quiet time for self-reflection or an adventurous escapade into the hills, Calm Cottage in Barog offers an comfort for all kinds of travelers, leaving them with cherished memories of their stay amidst the scenic beauty of Himachal Pradesh.

Services

Our Services

Accommodation

Cottages offer cozy and well-furnished rooms or cabins for guests to stay in, providing a home-like atmosphere.

Housekeeping:

Regular housekeeping services ensure that the rooms are clean, tidy, and well-maintained throughout the stay.

Room Service

Some cottages provide room service, allowing guests to order food and beverages to their rooms.

Food and dining

Many cottages offer meal options, such as complimentary breakfast or a restaurant on-site, while others may have a fully equipped kitchen for guests to prepare their meals.

Bedding and Linens

Cottages provide fresh bedding, towels, and other linens to ensure a comfortable sleep and personal hygiene.

Parking

On-site parking facilities ensure convenience for guests traveling with their vehicles.

Pet-Friendly

Some cottages may be pet-friendly, allowing guests to bring along their furry companions.

Security

Most cottages prioritize the safety and security of their guests with measures like surveillance cameras and staff presence
